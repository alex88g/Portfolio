Our Services

Android

Android-programmering är processen att utveckla mobila applikationer för Android-operativsystemet. Genom att använda Android-programmeringstjänster kan jag erbjuda och skapa appar för Android-enheter, inklusive smartphones, surfplattor, smarta klockor, TV-apparater och andra enheter som kör Android. Android-programmeringstjänster inkluderar utveckling av användargränssnitt, implementation av funktionalitet, integration av olika API:er och tjänster, testning, felsökning och distribution av appar till Google Play Store och andra marknadsplatser. Tjänster man kan utföra med olika programmeringsspråk och utvecklingsverktyg, dem tjänster jag kan erbjuda med programmeringsspråk är Kotlin med Android Studio verktyg.

iOS

iOS-programmering är processen att utveckla mobila applikationer för Apple-operativsystemet som används på iPhone, iPad, iPod touch och Apple Watch.Genom iOS-programmeringstjänster kan jag erbjuda och skapa appar som har en användarvänlig design, robust funktionalitet och god prestanda. Detta inkluderar utveckling av användargränssnitt med hjälp av Interface Builder och Auto Layout, implementation av funktionalitet med hjälp av objektorienterad programmering och Cocoa Touch Framework, integrering av olika API:er och tjänster, testning, felsökning och distribution av appar till Apple App Store.iOS-programmering kan utföras med olika programmeringsspråk, inklusive Swift och verktyg som Xcode.

iOS (SwiftUI): EatAtHomeExpress is a food delivery application that allows you to order food based on searching for restaurants, ordering food items, booking restaurant tables, and features two integrated Google Maps for location services. It includes detailed descriptions, a shopping cart for orders, settings for light and dark modes, a help center about food, and functionalities for uploading pictures and addressing customer cases. Additionally, users can create an account or reset their password. The database and all data are connected and managed through Firebase, ensuring secure and efficient data handling.
iOS (UIKit): TicTacToe is a game available in both single-player and multiplayer modes, featuring an appealing design. You can enjoy playing against the computer in single-player mode, or challenge a friend in multiplayer mode. The game includes a settings section that also displays scores.
iOS (UIKit): AutoFilex is an auto service platform designed for entrepreneurs, allowing them to subscribe by creating an account and utilizing services tailored to their needs. Each vehicle undergoes specific routines and steps, and we are here to simplify daily life for our clients. In the example shown in the images, entrepreneurs can create accounts for their employees after subscribing. These employees can then log in and register client vehicles. The platform offers standard services, and users can add or remove services as needed. Once the details of the client and vehicle are completed, employees move to the next step. After each process—whether it’s transport, testing, washing, repair, reconditioning, or others—the status is updated, and managers receive notifications to monitor service times and current status. Entrepreneurs benefit by tracking everything employees do within the app, receiving notifications, and having exclusive rights to create and manage employee accounts, including changing passwords and details. If an employee leaves the job, the entrepreneur can easily block their account, while all data remains saved in the database (Firebase). This app features login functionality, profiles for clients and entrepreneurs, a sidebar menu, tabs for different services, filtering for case tracking, and employee notifications. Additionally, the design is specifically tailored to match the app's theme, enhancing the overall user experience.
