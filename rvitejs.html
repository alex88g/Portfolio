React (Vite.js): Cinema Planet the web application is designed for online streaming subscriptions. Users can rent or purchase movies. Additionally, users can create an account to comment on and rate movies. They can also explore different categories for the latest releases, top upcoming movies, or series. The design is elegantly tailored to fit the app's theme and the customers' preferences and is fully responsive, ensuring optimal usability on both desktop and mobile devices.
