iOS (SwiftUI): EatAtHomeExpress is a food delivery application that allows you to order food based on searching for restaurants, ordering food items, booking restaurant tables, and features two integrated Google Maps for location services. It includes detailed descriptions, a shopping cart for orders, settings for light and dark modes, a help center about food, and functionalities for uploading pictures and addressing customer cases. Additionally, users can create an account or reset their password. The database and all data are connected and managed through Firebase, ensuring secure and efficient data handling.
iOS (UIKit): TicTacToe is a game available in both single-player and multiplayer modes, featuring an appealing design. You can enjoy playing against the computer in single-player mode, or challenge a friend in multiplayer mode. The game includes a settings section that also displays scores.
