Android (Kotlin): HiddenGem is an innovative application tailored to connect food enthusiasts with top-rated local restaurants. It enables restaurant owners to showcase their establishments, ensuring that users can effortlessly locate them. The platform streamlines user interaction by offering comprehensive features for account creation, login, and password recovery, alongside a personalized profile section for individual details. Within the 'Business' activity section, proprietors have the privilege to list new dining spots. Conversely, the 'Company' section is exclusively designed for owners to manage and review their own listings. This clear distinction ensures that company owners have enhanced control and exclusive access to additional app functionalities. For the general user base, HiddenGem offers an interactive map to explore and discover eateries. Users can delve into detailed reviews, engage through comments, and visually share their dining experiences by adding and uploading photos. Additionally, the application caters to user comfort with customizable settings for switching between dark and light themes. An integrated search bar further simplifies the process, providing a seamless way to navigate through the myriad of restaurant options available.
Android (Kotlin): Angry Cards is an exciting card game that offers two different modes: single-player, where users can play against the CPU, and multiplayer, where they can challenge a friend. The game includes customizable settings, allowing players to modify the rules based on their preferences. Additionally, players can access a built-in help center for detailed instructions on how to play the game.
